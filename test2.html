About Me

My Story

My journey started in software project management , where I spent over five years leading cross-functional teams and shipping products at scale. Along the way, I learned how to balance deadlines, stakeholders, and global delivery. But what excited me most wasn’t just delivery; it was why we were building something, how customers would use it and how data could show us if we got it right.

That curiosity pushed me to pursue my MBA in Information Systems & Analytics at the University at Buffalo, where I dove deeper into data-driven decision making, product design, and customer-centric strategy. From building attribution models in R to designing data warehouses and exploring product concepts , I found myself gravitating toward the intersection of technology, data, and user experience.

Now I want to bring that mix of execution, analytics , and customer empathy into product roles, because I believe the most impactful products are born when customer obsession meets data-driven insight.
