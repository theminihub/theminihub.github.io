About Me

My Story

My journey started in software project management , where I spent over five years leading cross-functional teams and shipping products at scale. Along the way, I learned how to balance deadlines, stakeholders, and global delivery. What excited me most was not just delivery. It was why we were building, how customers would use it, and how data could show us if we got it right.

That curiosity pushed me to pursue my MBA in Information Systems & Analytics at the University at Buffalo, where I dove deeper into data-driven decision making, product design, and customer-centric strategy. From building attribution models in R to designing data warehouses and exploring product concepts , I found the intersection of technology, data, and user experience.

Now I bring a mix of execution, analytics and customer empathy to product work, because the most impactful products happen when customer obsession meets data-informed insight.
